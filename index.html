Introducing Northstar 333

Your personal growth operating system.

Northstar 333 isn't another productivity tool. It's an intelligent companion built around one question: is the way you're living moving you toward the person you want to become?

Understands your goals

Not tasks on a list — the deeper direction underneath them. Northstar learns what you're actually building toward and holds it steady, even when daily life pulls at it.

Recognizes your patterns

The habits, decisions, and drift you can't see from inside your own week. It surfaces the patterns shaping your life — the ones serving you and the ones quietly working against you.

Keeps you aligned

Gentle, intelligent course-correction — not notifications, guilt, or streaks. When your actions and your intentions start to separate, Northstar helps you close the gap.

Why 333? It's the number for alignment — mind, body, and spirit moving in the same direction.

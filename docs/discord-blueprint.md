# Northstar 333 — Founding Community Blueprint (Discord)

The one step that needs a human: create the server (Discord → `+` → Create My Own → name it
**Northstar 333**) on the account of your choice, then create a permanent invite link
(Server Settings → Invites) and hand it to Winston. Everything below is ready to paste.

## Channel structure (keep it SMALL — empty channels kill young communities)

**✦ START HERE**
- `#welcome` — read-only. House rules + what this place is. (Copy below.)
- `#introductions` — every member's first post: name, where they're from, and their answer
  to "who are you trying to become?" (they already wrote it on the join form).

**✦ THE PRACTICE**
- `#sunday-check-in` — the weekly ritual. One pinned prompt, members reply in threads.
- `#wins-and-realignments` — "333 moments": caught yourself drifting and corrected. Celebrate both.

**✦ THE COMMUNITY**
- `#the-round-table` — general conversation.
- `#ask-the-founders` — direct line to Amanda & Sean.

That's it. Six channels. Add more only when one overflows.

## Roles
- **Founder** — Amanda, Sean
- **First 333** — every founding member (gold color, obviously)
- **Northstar** — the bot, when it arrives (Winston builds this in Phase 2)

## #welcome copy (paste as the pinned post)

> **Welcome to the 333.**
>
> This is the founding community of Northstar 333 — the first 333 people building
> a life of intention together, and shaping the product that will help millions more.
>
> **What we do here:**
> - Every Sunday, we check in: what moved you toward the person you're becoming,
>   what pulled you away, and the one intentional choice you're making this week.
> - We celebrate wins AND realignments. Catching yourself drifting is a victory here.
> - We tell the founders the truth. You're not an audience — you're a co-builder.
>
> **House rules:**
> 1. What's shared here stays here.
> 2. Encourage, don't fix. Ask before giving advice.
> 3. No selling, no recruiting, no MLM energy. Ever.
> 4. This is a growth community, not a substitute for professional care. If you're
>    struggling with your mental health, please reach out to a professional —
>    in the US, call or text **988** (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline). We care about you;
>    that's exactly why we point you to real help.
>
> Wear the reminder. Build the community. Help create a more aligned world.

## The Sunday check-in (pin in #sunday-check-in)

> **☀️ Sunday Alignment Check-in — 3 questions, 3 minutes:**
> 1. What did you do this week that moved you toward the person you're becoming?
> 2. What pulled you off course?
> 3. What's the ONE intentional choice you're committing to this week?
>
> Reply in a thread. Read one other person's check-in and leave them a ✦.

## Operating cadence (who does what)
- **Amanda (Founder / voice):** posts the Sunday prompt (or lets Winston schedule it),
  welcomes intros, hosts a monthly "333 Round Table" voice session when the group is ready.
- **Sean (Co-founder / ops):** necklace fulfillment from form submissions, member list.
- **Winston (automation):** processes join-form submissions from Gmail, sends welcome
  emails with the invite link, tracks member count vs 333 cap, and in Phase 2 builds the
  Northstar bot (onboarding Qs → monthly pattern reflections from each member's own check-ins).

## Phase gates — do not skip ahead
- **Gate 1 (33 engaged members):** are people still checking in at week 3 without prompting?
  If not, fix the ritual before growing.
- **Gate 2 (333 full):** close the doors, start the waitlist. Scarcity becomes real.
  Only THEN revisit paid membership / Founding Supporter contributions (entity + processor first).
